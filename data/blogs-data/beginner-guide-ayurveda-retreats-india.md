You've heard of Ayurveda. You've seen the word on face creams and yoga studio menus. But the real thing — a medically supervised retreat in Kerala where an Ayurvedic doctor designs your programme from scratch — is something else entirely. This guide covers what to expect, what the treatments involve, and how to tell a genuine retreat from a spa that's borrowed the vocabulary.

## What is Ayurveda?

Ayurveda is a 5,000-year-old system of natural healing that has its origins in the Vedic culture of India. Although suppressed during years of foreign occupation, Ayurveda has been enjoying a major resurgence in both its native land and throughout the world. Tibetan medicine and Traditional Chinese Medicine both have their roots in Ayurveda. 

Early Greek medicine also embraced many concepts originally described in the classical ayurvedic medical texts dating back thousands of years.
